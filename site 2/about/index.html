Meet the Godmother

Magali Dunkwu

Fifteen years. Three continents. One question that changes everything.

The truth nobody else will tell you.

I've spent fifteen years planning destination weddings across Morocco, Europe and West Africa under my company La Jet Fête — and it taught me one thing above all: the weddings that go wrong aren't about flowers. They're about whose day everyone thinks it is.

The Wedding Godmother is where I say the things I couldn't always say to a client's face — with warmth, a little mischief, and fifteen years of receipts. The book, the toolkit and the Circle all exist for one reason: so you can plan your wedding without losing your relationship, your money, or yourself along the way.

You may have heard me talking weddings on the BBC and CNN. Or you may have just been sent one of my verdicts by a friend who knows exactly what you're going through. Either way — welcome.

"Plan the marriage, not just the wedding."
